Follow The Flash!

Follow The Flash! is a one player memory game loosely based on Simon Says. This was my first project to build on the Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly using JavaScript, jQuery, HTML and CSS.

SnowFo

SnowFo was my second project at GA on the WDI course. The task was to build a multi-page app using authentication with a bonus of adding an API. SnowFo list's the best ski resorts that I have skied in Europe and Canada. I have also added an 'Add' feature where users can add their own resorts where they have skied. I used Balsamic as the wire frame and trello to help me plan.

RUNCH

RUNCH was my group project we created an app where professionals working in London could signup to ‘RUNCH’ and join local running groups which are happening in their area. This was particularly designed for people who wanted to get out of the office and go for a run on their lunch break. We integrated google Maps into this project.

RuPaul's Drag Race!

For my last project I decided to do something fun and to also show off how much I enjoy Front End and designing. I made a RuPaul’s Drag Race ‘fan page’. Using Ruby on Rails in the back with Authentication and AnglarJS in the front with scss.
